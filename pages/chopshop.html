The Ohio Chop Shop is a high-end, unique hair salon in Akron, Ohio that brings their clients the freshest styles and a fun atmosphere that keeps clients coming back again and again.

I led the research of competitors, navigation structure, page layouts, content strategy, and branding since the onset of the project in October 2017. I took into consideration the developed business goals and objectives to develop a strategy and vision for the user interface.

Starting November 2017, I led the front-end development for The Ohio Chop Shop across mobile, tablet and desktop platforms.

In October 2017, the owners of The Ohio Chop Shop realized they needed an online presence. They lacked a website to showcase who they were as a salon, provide useful information on their products and services, and compete with similar salons in their area.

My challenge was to create a seamless user interface that met the needs of the client while also providing a greater user experience. With an online presence I hoped to attract more business for The Ohio Chop Shop.

Research, design and development were broken into parallel workstreams. I led the research and design for all aspects related to content, page structure and best design practices.

I conducted a competitive analysis that would result in the website's sitemap and low fidelity wireframes. I conducted peer reviews to gather feedback on my wireframes that would develop into mockups and branding/styling. The mockups and brand guidelines were used as a guide through the front-end development phase of the website.

Through research and design, I curated 4 key elements to establish a user interface that offered a fun and upscale atmosphere that met the needs of the business:

  • Allow clients to book an appointment online

  • Showcase products sold and used in-store

  • Showcase stylists and their expertises

  • Highlight services and specials
