Emily Lavelle

Designer

I am a Cleveland based Front-End Developer, UI/UX Designer, and Graphic Designer creating effective and visually appealing pieces for organizations of all sizes. As a designer with complimentary skills, I am a problem solver who strives to deliver the best possible solutions while also catering to the client's needs.

My dream job would be to work for an organization that encourages me to dream big and think outside of the box. I want to be able to explore innovative ideas and grow as a designer and individual. Above all, I don't want to wake up 10 years from now and hate the work that I do.

I want to encourage, inspire, and provoke thought in others.

I am originally from the Pittsburgh, PA area, but have grown fond of Cleveland in the past 5 years. My most popular response to when people ask me, "Where do you want to move after college?" has been "I don't know." For me, the right job does not have an ideal location. As I reach the end of my college career, I am looking for entry-level positions and/or paid internships.

Outside of work, my hobbies include traveling, cooking (and eating), reading, playing sports, watching the latest show on Netflix or Hulu, and spending quality time with quality people.
