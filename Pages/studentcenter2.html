Mobile App

Eye Gaze

Role:UI/UX

Duration: March 2016 - November 2020

Boardmaker 7 Student Center is an app that brings personalized visuals and symbol-supported educational materials home. Assigned activities appear automatically in the learner’s account and can use custom settings for eye gaze, switch, mouse and touch.

Validated Designs

During the early stages of design, I worked in two-week increments where I would design a portion of the application then take it out for user feedback. This entailed in-person usability studies with a clickable prototype from Adobe Xd. The feedback was used to validate the designs or highlight areas that needed to be revisited.

To gather feedback, each usability study would involve a prototype and a series of tasks for the user to complete. There would be a moderator and at least one note-taker. The tasks would be rated by our metrics of success. In order for a design to pass, the average on our metrics of success scale would have to be 70% or greater for each task.
