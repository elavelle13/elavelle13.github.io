Boardmaker 7 Editor is the world’s leading symbol-based special education solution. This application supports learning and communication in the classroom, therapy room, or home with a trove of customizable material and Picture Communication Symbols® (PCS).

Final Touches

After determining the desired workflows, I worked on polishing the UI and prioritizing the backlog with team members to determine what would make MVP and what would be included in a future update.

When Boardmaker 7 Editor launched, the team took a step back to address any major performance bugs that needed to be addressed quickly. As the product stables and we launch TD Updater, the team will regroup to determine the next steps and future updates.
