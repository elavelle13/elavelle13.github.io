Mobile App

Eye Gaze

Role:UI/UX

Duration: March 2018 - November 2020

Boardmaker 7 Student Center is an app that brings personalized visuals and symbol-supported educational materials home. Assigned activities appear automatically in the learner’s account and can use custom settings for eye gaze, switch, mouse and touch.

Final Touches

After determining the desired workflows, I worked on polishing the UI and adding the remainder of the project requirements. The outstanding requirements that were missing from the early stages of design were some settings that weren't known at the time and small details like adding the user's name under Settings to indicate what account they're signed into.

These requirements were discovered during development or final usability studies. Typically, I do not conduct usability studies that far into a project, but there was a desire to get the product in front of more users to get final feedback. Any areas that we felt needed to be addressed were either prioritized to be completed before launch or in a future update.
