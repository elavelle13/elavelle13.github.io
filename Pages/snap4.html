Software

Role:UI/UX

Duration: April 2021 - July 2021

TD Snap is a flexible software offering professionals and families a choice of AAC solutions. It comes with a set of powerful tools and resources to empower people with speech and language disabilities to communicate.

Final Touches

After determining the desired workflows, I worked on polishing the UI and updating messaging when needed. I made sure that all features that were removed and slated for a later release would be easy to integrate back into the UI.

Reflecting back on the project, I felt that I had adapted well to jumping into the middle of a project with a new team with their own set of processes and guidelines. I feel confident that I produced the best user experience possible given the unforeseen circumstances that occurred due to COVID-19.
