Software

Role:UI/UX

Duration: April 2021 - July 2021

TD Snap is a flexible software offering professionals and families a choice of AAC solutions. It comes with a set of powerful tools and resources to empower people with speech and language disabilities to communicate.

Early Concepts

Early concepts of the onboarding experience were in the works before I was assigned to the project. When joining the team, I familiarised myself with the workflows and design guidelines already established in TD Snap. This would later help me incorporate new designs that still felt familiar to their users.

With most of the early work done before I joined the team, my main focus was improving the UI wherever needed and making adjustments to the workflow for newly added design requirements.
